Privacy Policy

Patois does not collect personal data.

  • No account required
  • No analytics SDK
  • No remote API calls for translation
  • No data selling or sharing

All conversion happens locally in your browser. Local stats are used only to help improve dictionary suggestions and can be exported by the user.

Permissions used

storage for settings + conversion stats. <all_urls> to convert visible text on webpages.
