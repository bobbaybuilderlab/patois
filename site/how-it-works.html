How it works

  1. Extension scans visible text nodes on a page.
  2. Matches words against a seeded top-1000 English dictionary.
  3. Converts about 50% of eligible words to keep readability.
  4. Tracks local conversion counts so we can grow the dictionary.
  5. You can export growth suggestions as JSON from popup.

Auto-grow dictionary

We track which words are converted most, export them, review manually, then add better Patois mappings in updates. Human approval keeps quality high and avoids bad slang mapping.
